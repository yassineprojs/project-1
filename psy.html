FAQ

Any Questions? Look Here

Is an online psychiatrist the same as an in-person psychiatrist?

Online psychiatrists are just like in-person psychiatrists. Whether in-person or virtually, providers specialize in diagnosing and treating mental health conditions. The main difference is the method of delivery — you'll be seeing an online psychiatrist on screen rather than sitting in a doctor's office. Our providers can conduct sessions from wherever you're most comfortable, with the additional benefit of being able to prescribe mental health medication online.

How much is an online psychiatrist?

Costs for an appointment with an online psychiatrist can vary depending on where you live and sometimes on what mental health specialty you need. Generally an initial assessment with a psychiatrist costs more than follow-up sessions because that initial session will be longer, include a mental health evaluation, and a preliminary treatment plan.

What can I expect from my first psychiatry appointment?

The first appointment is all about getting to know you. Your online psychiatrist will ask some questions about your medical and psychological history, including information about any medications you're taking or mental health symptoms you're experiencing, and why you are seeking psychiatric care. From there, they will make their recommendations for further treatment, including any prescription medications.

How do I get my medication?

You can pick up your psychiatric medication from your local pharmacy. If you receive mail order delivery through your insurance, you will also be able to receive your medication through delivery as well.
